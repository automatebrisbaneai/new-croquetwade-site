Content

From voice recording to statewide newsletter. Automatically.

Dictate a match report on your phone. It knows your name and 200 croquet terms. It flows through editorial, hits the news site, gets picked up by the newsletter, and drives readers back into the ecosystem. Every article has a call to action.

share.croquetqld.org. Voice-to-text knows 200+ croquet terms and 157 QLD player names. Photos, video, match reports. No retyping.
Quill editor, AI title and excerpt suggestions, approval workflow. Quality gate before publication.
news.croquetqld.org. Structured data, 7 hub pages, built for AI search visibility.
1,735 members geocoded. Every article drives readers back to the ecosystem. Auto-scanned from news.
tv.croquetwade.com. PeerTube + Cloudflare R2. All QLD croquet video collected.
